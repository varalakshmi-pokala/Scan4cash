About Scan4Cash

Scan4Cash is an eco-friendly initiative to keep our villages and cities clean while helping people earn money from recyclable waste. Instead of throwing plastic, metal, glass, or old clothes, users can upload their waste details and sell them to collectors. This reduces pollution, protects animals, and promotes recycling.
Minimum 500g required per submission.

Mission

Our mission is to keep the environment clean by helping people recycle waste and earn money sustainably.

Vision

A cleaner planet where every piece of waste becomes an opportunity for income and sustainability.

Values

Sustainability, Community, Innovation, and Financial Empowerment through responsible waste management.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of waste can I recycle?

We accept plastics, paper, cardboard, metals, glass, electronics, and organic waste. Check our guidelines for specific items.

How do I get paid?

Payment is processed within 48 hours after collection verification via mobile money or bank transfer.

Is there a minimum waste quantity?

Yes, minimum of 5kg for most materials. Larger quantities earn better rates.

Do you pick up from anywhere?

We service urban and suburban areas. Use our location detector to check availability in your area.
